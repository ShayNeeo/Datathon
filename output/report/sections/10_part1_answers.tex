\subsection{Key Technical Observation}
Our analysis shows a clear divide between transactional efficiency and customer retention. While the 55+ age group (Q6) represents the most loyal cohort, the overall business is driven by the East region (Q7) and the Streetwear category. The high promotion rate (Q5) combined with the sizing friction (Q3, Q9) suggests that while marketing is effectively bringing customers to the door, the product fulfillment stage is failing to maximize the customer lifetime value (CLV).
