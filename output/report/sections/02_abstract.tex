% Abstract

\begin{abstract}
This report details a strategic audit of a decade-long (2012--2022) longitudinal dataset from a leading Vietnamese fashion e-commerce enterprise. The business has achieved massive scale, with a cumulative revenue of \textbf{16.43 billion VND} across 650,000 orders. However, this audit reveals that top-line growth is masking severe operational leaks. Using forensic data linking, we expose a collapse in customer retention, a loss-leader strategy that destroys basket profit, an inelastic logistics response that wastes shipping spend, and a predictable seasonal demand shock that strains inventory. We conclude with immediate prescriptive actions to plug these leaks, prioritizing margin discipline and loyalty over simple acquisition volume.
\end{abstract}
