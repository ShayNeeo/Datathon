\begin{abstract}
This report details a strategic audit of a decade-long (2012--2022) longitudinal dataset from a leading Vietnamese fashion e-commerce enterprise. With a cumulative revenue of \textbf{16.43 billion VND} across 650,000 orders, the business displays mature product-market fit in the Streetwear category (80\% of revenue) but faces significant operational headwinds. Our analysis identifies a critical sizing friction point, where 35\% of returns are attributed to fitting issues, and a promotion paradox that currently cannibalizes average order value (AOV) by 21\%. We conclude with five prescriptive strategic imperatives aimed at optimizing the 2024--2025 revenue trajectory, specifically leveraging the identified mid-week (Wednesday) and seasonal (May) demand peaks.
\end{abstract}
