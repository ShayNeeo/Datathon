## 2. Potential Discussions & Breakthroughs

### 🚀 Breakthrough 1: The "Return Crisis" in Specific Segments
If the **Return Friction Matrix** shows high returns in specific categories (e.g., Streetwear) due to "wrong_size", it confirms the sizing friction issue noted in the context.
* **Discussion**: Should we implement AI-driven sizing recommendations?

### 💰 Breakthrough 2: The Promotion Paradox
If the **Promotion Impact** chart shows that while revenue increases with promos, the **AOV (Average Order Value)** actually decreases, the business is essentially "buying" revenue at the cost of quality.
* **Discussion**: Shift from flat discounts to "Bundle-based" promotions to boost AOV.

### 📈 Breakthrough 3: The Conversion-Traffic Gap
If the **Digital Funnel Efficiency** shows high sessions but plummeting conversion rates during peak months (May), it suggests a UX/performance bottleneck or inventory unavailability.
* **Discussion**: Optimize website performance for mobile and ensure real-time inventory visibility.

### 🛡️ Breakthrough 4: Demographic Goldmines
If the **Demographic Wealth** heatmap identifies a specific age/gender cohort (e.g., 18-25 females) as the primary revenue driver, marketing can be hyper-targeted.
* **Discussion**: Tailor the acquisition strategy and product design to this "Super-Segment".

### 📉 Breakthrough 5: Stockout-Revenue Correlation
The **Inventory Risk Analysis** can reveal if certain high-revenue products are frequently out of stock, leading to direct revenue leakage.
* **Discussion**: Implement predictive reordering and safety stock optimization for "Hero" products.
